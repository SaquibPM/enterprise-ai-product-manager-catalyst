## VISION: Autonomous Enterprise Procurement

### What is Autonomous Procurement?

Today's procurement is manual:
- Legal teams spend 4-6 hours reviewing each contract
- Procurement managers manually check supplier risk
- Finance teams spend 20+ hours per month on spend analysis and forecasting
- Compliance teams manually audit contracts and spending

**Tomorrow's autonomous procurement** (our vision):
- AI reviews contracts in seconds, flags risks automatically
- AI scores supplier risk in real-time
- AI forecasts spend patterns with 95%+ accuracy
- AI automates compliance audits (SOX, HIPAA, FAR, GDPR)

**Outcome**: Procurement teams shift from manual work to strategic partnerships (risk mitigation, supplier development, cost optimization).

## RISKS & MITIGATION

### Risk 1: Competitive Response (Medium Risk)

**Risk**: Coupa/Jaggr launch AI features in 2026, eroding our differentiation

**Mitigation**:
1. Move fast (AI contract scoring GA Aug 2026, before competitors)
2. Build compliance moat (10+ vertical-specific rules by Q4, harder to replicate)
3. Achieve 95%+ accuracy early (hard to catch up on accuracy once customers trust us)
4. Patent compliance AI (defensibility through IP)

**Timeline**: 12-month moat is real; compete on execution

---

### Risk 2: AI Accuracy Falls Short (Medium Risk)

**Risk**: Model achieves only 85% accuracy instead of 90%+, limiting customer adoption

**Mitigation**:
1. Conservative thresholds (only flag high-confidence risks)
2. Manual override workflow (legal team confirms/rejects)
3. Feedback loop (collect legal team overrides, retrain monthly)
4. Fallback to RAG (rules-based system ensures accuracy floor)

**Expected outcome**: Achieve 90%+ by Sept with customer feedback

---

### Risk 3: Larger Deal Cycles Than Expected (Low Risk)

**Risk**: Government/healthcare deals take 6-9 months (vs. expected 4-5 months)

**Mitigation**:
1. Early pilot programs (shorten time to value)
2. Reference customers (case studies accelerate sales)
3. Vertical specialists (dedicated sales engineers for healthcare/gov)

**Contingency**: Adjust 2027 targets down 20% if deal cycles longer

---

### Risk 4: LLM API Costs Unsustainable (Low Risk)

**Risk**: LLM vendor (OpenAI) raises prices 10x+, breaks unit economics

**Mitigation**:
1. Multi-vendor strategy (support Anthropic, Cohere as fallbacks)
2. Monitor API costs quarterly, switch if needed
3. Plan for fine-tuned model by 2027 (long-term, if needed)

**Contingency**: Not expected to materialize; multiple vendors available
