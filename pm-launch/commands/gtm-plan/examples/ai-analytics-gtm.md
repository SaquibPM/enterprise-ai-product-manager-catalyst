## WORKSTREAM 1: Sales Enablement

### Sales Messaging Framework

**Opening Message** (First 30 seconds):
"Your legal team spends 4-6 hours reviewing each contract manually. With our AI-powered contract risk scorer, that drops to 1 hour. You catch compliance violations automatically. It's the only solution on the market that does this."

**Value Props by Segment**:

#### Finance Segment
- Reduce contract review time: 4-6 hrs → 1 hour
- Ensure SOX compliance: automatic audit trail of all reviews
- Speed up M&A diligence: pre-screen 200+ contracts in 1 week instead of 1 month
- ROI: 5 FTEs saved × $150k/yr = $750k savings

#### Healthcare Segment
- Ensure HIPAA compliance: flag contracts with unacceptable data handling terms
- Ensure FDA compliance: identify supplier qualification risks
- Reduce regulatory risk: documented review process for audits
- ROI: Avoid 1 compliance violation ($500k+ fine) = break-even in first contract review

#### Government/Defense Segment
- Ensure FAR/DFARS compliance: automatically flag prohibited supplier types
- Reduce past performance risk: supplier stability scoring
- Speed up bid response: review RFP-related contracts in days vs. weeks
- ROI: Win 1 additional contract ($5M+) = massive return

## Risk Mitigation

**Risk 1: AI Model Accuracy Too Low**
- Mitigation: Use conservative thresholds (90%+ confidence), require legal review
- Fallback: Position as "assist tool" not "decision tool"

**Risk 2: Legal Team Adoption Slow**
- Mitigation: Target contracts with clear compliance rules (healthcare first)
- Fallback: Offer white-glove onboarding for early customers

**Risk 3: Competitive Response**
- Mitigation: Move fast (GA by Aug 31), emphasize AI-first positioning
- Fallback: Develop government compliance rules ASAP (differentiation)
